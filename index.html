PhD Candidate in Architecture and Civil Engineering, CityU / 香港城市大学

Research Interests

Working under the guidance of Chair Professor Qiu-Sheng Li (李秋胜), I focus on high-fidelity fluid-structure interaction simulations for wind turbines. My research combines CFD and FSI to capture detailed aerodynamic and structural behavior in complex, real-world operating conditions.

Core areas: high-precision wind turbine FSI simulation, CFD, FSI.

Research tools: OpenFOAM, PreCICE, CalculiX, OpenFAST.
